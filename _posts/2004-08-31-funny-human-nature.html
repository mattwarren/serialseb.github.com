As I’m very much in a ranting mood…

A few weeks ago, bored one night, I was cruising a wiki somewhere on the internet. One of the page was talking about a meeting where they were to discuss an issue I was interested in. Not providing any contact information and not showing any will to have any feedback, I thought, well, let’s wiki the question, hopefully it’ll be taken into account and I could even get an answer. After all, we are all going for the same goal right?

The meeting happened but it completely slipped my mind. I did go there again today, to read the summary of what was discussed and decided. My question brought no answer, but they did decide to act upon something:

“Prevent people from outside our group to edit our Wiki”.

I’m speechless. And a bit depressed..
