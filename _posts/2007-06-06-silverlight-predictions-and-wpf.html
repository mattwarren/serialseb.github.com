A lot could be said about the addition of UIPropertyMetadata against FrameworkPropertyMetadata. Rule of thumb if you're using WPF, unless you know the difference between the UIElement and the FrameworkElement levels, stick with the Framework layer. In this case, for WPF FrameworkPropertyMetadata is acceptable, for other toolkits only PropertyMetadata would be more accurate.
